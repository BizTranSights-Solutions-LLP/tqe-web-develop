Why are multiple Sportsbooks important to have?

Line shopping is incredibly important to a sports bettor’s success and R.O.I. Not all books will have the same line on a game. Half points cause -10 of juice and to save that money on bets time and time again will help you grow your bankroll. One book may have the Patriots as -7, while another book may have the Patriots as -6.5. That half point can be the difference between you cashing your ticket and you pushing your bet.

Not only will different books have different lines on games, but they will also have different odds on games. The standard odds on a bet is -110. With this juice, you need to hit 52.4% to be profitable as a sports bettor. Some books may have the line juiced to -115 or even -120 while another book could have the line at -105. What this means is that on one book you may need to risk $120 to win $100 while on another book you only need to risk $105. This is important for money lines as well as getting a team at +220 instead of +200 makes a huge difference as well. Better odds apply to all bets beyond straight bets and money lines, futures are important as well. Whenever you are going to tie your money up for a long term bet, you want to make sure you maximize your potential return. Often sportsbook differentials in lines are biggest on futures where a team might be 10-1 on one site, but 15-1 on another. That’s a difference of 500 dollars on a one hundred dollar bet!

Having multiple sportsbooks is critical to your chances to be profitable long term as a bettor. Of course, another way to be a profitable long-term bettor is to sign up at TQE, where across all NFL games we covered the spread 59.6% of the time and 68% on our best bets!

A new, fun thing that sportsbooks are introducing is ‘Daily Odds Boost.’ This gives you a boost in the odds on different events of the night across multiples sports. If you wanted to bet that Joel Embiid would score over 20 points on DraftKings, but Sugarhouse has it as an odds boost play and you get 2-1 instead of even money, it’s important that you have an account at multiple books. The more sports books the better, but three should be the minimum a serious sports bettor has.
