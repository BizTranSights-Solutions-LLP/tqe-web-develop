Meet the Team

Todd Gross, Founder and CEO

Todd founded TheQuantEdge with a vision to help sports enthusiasts make money by making smarter betting and daily fantasy decisions. He previously ran Global Commodities Options Trading for Morgan Stanley and then founded Hudson Capital Group, and was awarded the best performing commodities hedge fund manager by BarclayHedge several times. Todd has brought the expertise in quantitative analysis and machine learning he used in the financial markets to build TQE's sports betting and fantasy platform. Todd is a graduate of the University of Pennsylvania's Management and Technology Program, including degrees from the Wharton School of Business and School of Engineering (where he has served on the board of overseers). His favorite sport is baseball and his favorite team is the New York Yankees.
