Sports

Contests

Contests include Stat Shootout, Rapid Fire, and Over/Under.

Stat Fire: Choose a list of players and a stat goal for those players to exceed. For example, in order to cash in the NBA “Scoring Champ,” you pick any three players in that particular game that will score more points than the line provided. Monkey Knife Fight provides three lines: one where you can 2x, 3x, or 5x your money. The higher the payout, the higher the line.

Rapid Fire: Depending on the size of the payout, select a team by choosing the highest scoring player in multiple head-to-head matchups and statistical categories. Some players are offered a handicap, which will be seen in green to the right of the players name with a + and whatever the handicap number is. Depending on the amount of players selected, you have the opportunity to 1.68x your money all the way up to 19x your money.

Over/Under: This contest is the most straightforward. Simply choose your team of two to five players and select the over or under you prefer for the given statistical category. Payouts range from 1.58x to 19x your money.
