DraftKings’ Sportsbook is a Leader in Betting and Daily Fantasy Sports

Money lines are fairly juiced, and DraftKings offers fair, industry standard DFS sports betting lines. One outstanding feature that DK offers is the ability to get out of a wager before it is over. If you have a side and worried they might blow a lead, you can cash out at a predetermined value and move on, not needing to wait for the game to end.

Deposit Options – For online betting, DraftKings offers numerous deposit options, including credit cards, bank transfers, PayPal, skill, and prepaid cards. It is a simple process that can be completed in seconds.

Mobile App – DraftKings has first-rate mobile betting apps. The home page loads fast and gives you a with a fingerprint option to skip log-in steps. DK locates you quickly and has a user-friendly interface. It’s easy to find the game you want to bet, the day’s promotions and a wide range of sports betting options including a page dedicated to live wagers.

Easy Withdrawal – DraftKings offers fast withdrawal options. You can directly deposit into your account, have the funds sent to PayPal, or even have your initial deposit from your credit card refunded. Withdrawals can be completed fast, and the money will be in your account within 24 hours.

Customer Service – DK offers excellent customer service. You can email, call, live chat, or reach out on social media.  Agents are available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

Daily Odds Boost – Every day, DraftKings has a section of its sports betting website and app devoted to daily odds boost. It offers boosts to lines, player props, and parlays and lets you know what the odds were before the boost. With multiple options on different types of bets, an easy-to-navigate page, and consistent updates throughout the day, TQE gives DK an excellent rating for the daily odds boost on its website.

Deposit Bonus – A free bet of up to $500 in an excellent deposit bonus. Make sure you understand that it’s your initial wager that qualifies for the matching free bet. Free Plays – DraftKings gives you additional free bets when you refer friends and will offer promotions from time to time. Most often, you’ll receive free bets around major sporting events or launches of new products. Both are emailed to you and easy to find on the app.
