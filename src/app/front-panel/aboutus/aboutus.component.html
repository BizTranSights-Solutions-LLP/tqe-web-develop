Meet The Team

Todd Gross, Founder and CEO

Todd founded TheQuantEdge with a vision to help sports enthusiasts make money by making smarter betting and daily fantasy decisions. He previously ran Global Commodities Options Trading for Morgan Stanley and then founded Hudson Capital Group, and was awarded the best performing commodities hedge fund manager by BarclayHedge several times. Todd has brought the expertise in quantitative analysis and machine learning he used in the financial markets to build TQE’s sports betting and fantasy platform.

Todd is a graduate of the University of Pennsylvania’s Management and Technology Program, including degrees from the Wharton School of Business and School of Engineering (where he has served in the board of overseers). His favorite sport is baseball and his favorite team is the New York Yankees.

Marek Fludzinski, Chief Scientist

Marek oversees TQE’s approach to analytics and performance. Marek has over 25 years of experience in finance, technology, and physics. Previously, he was the founder and CEO of Thales Fund Management, a $2.5 billion quantitative hedge fund. He has also held leadership roles in the research groups of Citadel and DE Shaw.

Marek graduated from Columbia University with a Bachelor of Science in Physics and from Princeton University with a Ph.D. in Theoretical Physics. Marek is an avid ice hockey player.
